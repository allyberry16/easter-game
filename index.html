How to Play

Catch the falling blessings in your basket to earn points and level up.

💖 Blessings: Catch them to gain points.
👑 Golden blessings: Worth more points.
☁️ Obstacles: If you catch one, you lose a heart.
⚡ Lightning: At harder levels, lightning may strike and take a heart.
❤️ Lives: Lose all hearts and the game ends.
🎨 Themes: Classic and Bunny themes change the blessings and basket style.
